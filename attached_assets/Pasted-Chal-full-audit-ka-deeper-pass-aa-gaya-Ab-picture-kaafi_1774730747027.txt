# Executive summary
**infiniterankers.io technically crawlable hai, but SEO-wise overbuilt aur quality-risk mode mein lag rahi hai.**

## Straight verdict
- **Indexation possible hai** — site blocked nahi hai
- **SSR / crawlable HTML likely working enough**
- **Primary issue SSR nahi lagta**
- **Primary issues are:**
  1. sitemap pollution
  2. thin / repetitive page patterns
  3. weak entity clarity
  4. broad topical spread
  5. likely low trust-to-page-count ratio
  6. possible doorway-style expansion risk

Google ke liye “crawlable” aur “worth indexing” do alag cheezein hain.
