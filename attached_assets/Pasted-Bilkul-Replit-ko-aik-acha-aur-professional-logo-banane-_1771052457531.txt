Replit (ya kisi bhi Image AI) ke liye Logo Prompt:
Create a professional and modern logo for a website named "Infinite Rankers".

Key elements to include or suggest:
1.  **Icon:** A stylized, abstract symbol representing concepts like:
    * **Infinity/Continuity:** Loop, Mobius strip, or an interconnected pathway.
    * **Growth/Ranking:** Upward arrow, ladder, steps, or a growing graph.
    * **Achievement/Excellence:** Star, crown, or a subtle upward movement.
    * **Digital/Data:** Subtle pixelation, circuit board lines, or abstract data flow.
2.  **Color Palette:**
    * **Primary:** Deep blues, vibrant greens, or professional purples (symbolizing growth, stability, intelligence).
    * **Accent:** A touch of gold, silver, or bright teal (for excellence, innovation, or a futuristic feel).
    * Avoid overly bright or childish colors.
3.  **Typography:**
    * Clean, sans-serif font (e.g., Montserrat, Lato, Open Sans) for "Infinite Rankers".
    * The font should be easily readable and convey professionalism.
    * Consider making "Infinite" slightly bolder or more visually distinct than "Rankers" (or vice versa) if it enhances the design.
4.  **Style:**
    * Flat design or subtle gradients.
    * Minimalist, sleek, and scalable (looks good small or large).
    * No overly complex details or 3D effects that might look dated.
    * The logo should look good on both light and dark backgrounds.
5.  **Variations:** Provide 2-3 distinct concepts or variations.

**Target Audience:** Professionals, gamers, content creators, or anyone seeking to improve their rankings or performance.

**Output Request:**
Show the logo in isolation, and then as if integrated into a website header.
