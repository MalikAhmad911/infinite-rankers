Haan — agar tum **sirf services-focused website** rakhna chahte ho, toh yeh actually better ho sakta hai.

**Blunt answer:**
Abhi tumhari site ka issue zyada pages hona nahi, **zyada mixed-purpose pages** hona hai:
- old branded pages
- overlapping service pages
- maybe weak city pages
- broad generic pages
- some legacy SEO pages
- some new pages

Agar tum clear decision le lo ke:
**“site = service pages first, baqi old clutter remove”**
toh indexing aur clarity dono improve ho sakte hain.

---

# Best approach agar sirf services rakhni hain

## Keep:
### Core pages
- Home
- About
- Services
- Pricing
- Contact
- Portfolio / Case Studies
- Blog (optional, but useful)
- Privacy
- Terms

### Service cluster pages
Sirf woh pages rakho jo real money pages hain.

Example:
- /seo-agency
- /local-seo
- /google-ads
- /meta-ads
- /ai-calling-agent
- /ai-receptionist
- /ai-lead-qualification
- /ai-appointment-booking
- /crm-automation
- /workflow-automation
- /website-development
- /landing-page-development

But even here:
**30+ pages mat rakho unless har page genuinely unique ho.**

---

# Remove / merge / redirect:
## Remove old clutter like:
- old branded landing pages
- duplicate service pages
- thin pages
- pages with same intent
- outdated experiments
- old “Infinite Rankers + service” pages if generic service page already exists

Examples from what I saw:
- `/infinite-rankers-seo-services`
- `/infinite-rankers-paid-advertising`
- `/infinite-rankers-ai-automation`
- `/infinite-rankers-agency`

Agar inka equivalent better generic page already hai, then:
- **merge content**
- choose one primary URL
- old one → **301 redirect**

---

# Important:
## “Remove” ka matlab seedha delete nahi hota
SEO-wise best flow yeh hota hai:

### If page has no value:
- remove from nav/internal links
- 301 to closest relevant page

### If page has some useful content:
- best content merge into target page
- then 301 redirect old URL

### If page useless + no close equivalent:
- 410 or noindex/remove, but 301 usually better if relevant destination exists

# My recommendation on page count
## Ideal starting point:
### Keep around:
- 8 to 12 strong service pages max
- not 30+ thin ones

Yeh zyada healthy hoga.

Because:
- easier internal linking
- easier uniqueness
- easier indexing
- clearer topical authority
- less cannibalization

---

# Kya remove karna chahiye first?
## First-pass removals:
1. duplicate branded service pages
2. overlapping intent pages
3. thin city pages
4. legacy pages not linked strongly
5. anything old that doesn't fit current positioning

---

# Rule for deciding page keep vs remove

## Keep only if page has:
- unique keyword intent
- unique user need
- unique copy
- unique proof
- internal links pointing to it
- actual commercial value

## Remove/merge if page is:
- same service, different wording
- same template with keyword swaps
- old positioning
- weak content
- no proof
- no internal support
- no clear SERP intent match

---

# Example
If you already have:
- `/seo-agency`
and also
- `/infinite-rankers-seo-services`

Then likely:
- keep `/seo-agency`
- merge best content
- 301 `/infinite-rankers-seo-services` → `/seo-agency`

Same logic for ads / AI automation / agency pages.

---

# Important warning
Aik cheez ka khayal:

**Sab kuch hata dena bhi galat hoga.**

Because site ko still chahiye:
- trust pages
- legal pages
- about/company info
- proof pages/case studies
- contact/demo pages

Sirf service pages + kuch bhi nahi = thoda weak lag sakta hai.

So “sirf services” ka practical version yeh hona chahiye:
**service-led site**, not literally services-only.
