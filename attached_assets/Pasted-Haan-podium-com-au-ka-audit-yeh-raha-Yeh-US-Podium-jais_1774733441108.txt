# 13) Weaknesses / issues

Ab honest part:

## A) Some URLs are ugly / inconsistent
Biggest obvious one:
- `/automations-2`

That screams legacy slug / migration mess.

Also structure mixed lagta hai:
- `/product/reviews` → `/reviews`
- `/payments`
- `/ai-assistant`
- `/third-party-websites`
- `/marketplace`

Architecture workable hai, but cleaner ho sakta tha.

## B) Some pages feel thinner than flagship pages
Payments and automations extracts mein clarity hai, but some pages felt:
- shorter
- more FAQ-like
- less dense with proof than reviews page

## C) Marketplace page looked weak in fetch
`/marketplace` readable extraction weak thi.  
Maybe rendering issue hai, maybe page thin hai.

## D) AI Assistant page extraction looked cluttered
Likely due to frontend/rendering, but content output mein nav/footer noise zyada aa gaya.  
Still page intent clear tha.

# 18) What I’d improve if this were my site
If I were auditing it for action:

## Priority improvements:
1. clean up URL inconsistencies
2. rename ugly legacy slugs like `/automations-2`
3. strengthen thinner product pages with more proof
4. ensure internal linking between:
   - blog → product pages
   - customer stories → relevant product pages
   - business types → matching product pages
5. reduce nav/footer clutter if affecting readability/rendering
6. improve marketplace/integration visibility if page is thin

## Not perfect, but strong.
Main issues:
- some messy URLs
